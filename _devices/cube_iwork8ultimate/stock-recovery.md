**How to restore stock recovery:**

1. Download suitable zip for your device below and copy it to your device's sdcard.
2. Boot into [TWRP recovery](/devices/cube_iwork8ultimate/TWRP).
3. Install zip in TWRP.
4. Reboot recovery.
